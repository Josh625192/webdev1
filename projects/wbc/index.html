History

The world baseball classic first began in 2006. It was sort of a replacement for the Olympics since they knew that the sport was being removed from the Summer Games. Just like the World Cup, the event happens every four years except this time it will be after six years due to the pandemic. The 2017 World Baseball Classic was one of the most watched sporting events in history. It provided lots of iconic moments and memories for years to come which is why the upcoming one has a lot of hype surrounding it. Other than the fact that the United States will be very stacked.

Venues

There will be six different venues for this years classic. The Qualifier Pool A will be played in Germany from September 16th through the 21st. The Qualifier Pool B will be played in Panama from September 30th through October 5th. The group in Pool A will play in Taiwan from March 8th through March 13th. The group in Pool B will play in Japan from March 9th through March 13th. The group in Pool C will play in Arizona from March 11th through March 15th. The group in Pool D will play in Florida from March 11th through March 15th. Quarterfinal No. 1 will be played in Tokyo while Quarterfinal No. 2 will be played in Miami. The semifinals and the finals will also be played in Miami
